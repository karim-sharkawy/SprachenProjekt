Motivation

Hello! I'm Karim El-Sharkawy, and I'm excited to present the map below, showcasing languages from around the globe, including those that are alive, endangered, and extinct. Creating this map was a step towards enhancing my parsing skills and gaining insight into both backend and frontend development. I'm open to any suggestions for improvement! Beyond the valuable experience gained, my primary motivation stems from a verse in the Quran. As a Muslim, the Quran encourages me to seek knowledge about different cultures, and one way I aspire to fulfill this is by learning their languages.
